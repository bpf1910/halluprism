Stawberry Week

Remember when that map with each country's unique denomination for menstruation went around? No? Well, my chaotic self sure does and this was a result derived directly from it.

Life was never the same when I found out about the term "Strawberry Week" (German btw). It was probably the sweetest expression I've ever heard associated to that time of the month. Using it always lifts my mood because of the ridiculous scenario it builds up in my head, reason why I decided to officially translate it into art (you're welcome).
