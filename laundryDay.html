Laundry Day

Concept Summary:

  1960's New York: In the span of a week, Vivienne goes through a cycle of dress-picking and partying, the rags progressively pilling up in her laundry basket. By its end, she notices her wardrobe’s ran out, meaning it’s time for her to stop and reflect on her excessive lifestyle, and face the consequences it brings her – here portrayed in the form of washing her clothes.
